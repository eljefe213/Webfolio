Project Overview

My project is a personal version of the classic arcade game 1942. The game is packed with a wide range of functionalities that enhance the gaming experience. Players will have access to several features such as the score, and the ability to move in different directions including left, right, up and down. In addition, the game will also feature sound effects that create a realistic and immersive experience for players. Other functionalities will be included to enhance the gameplay, making it more engaging and exciting for players. With all these features, the game promises to be a thrilling and entertaining experience for gamers of all ages.
